Pallavi

Pallavi made her acting debut with the 2015 Malayalam film Premam, which was the second highest-grossing Malayalam film at that point. She has since established herself in South Indian cinema with acclaimed performances in films like Kali (2016), Fidaa (2017), Middle Class Abbayi (2017), Maari 2 (2018), Athiran (2019), Paava Kadhaigal (2020), Love Story (2021), Shyam Singha Roy (2021) and Gargi (2022).
